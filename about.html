About

The person behind the analysis

Hey - I'm Aaryan. I've been watching football obsessively for years, the kind of watching where you're tracking the left back's positioning while everyone else is just waiting for the next goal. The Final Third is where I put that to use: proper pre-match and post-match breakdowns, built on confirmed lineups and actual tactical reasoning, not vibes and stat dumps.

I started this during the 2026 World Cup as a side project - something that sits at the edge of a hobby and something I can point to professionally. If you care about why a game went the way it did rather than just what the score was, you're in the right place.
The approach

Every prediction is built on confirmed lineups, not assumed ones.

Analysis gets updated live as the match unfolds - goals, substitutions, disallowed decisions, tactical shifts. The pre-match post and post-match review are part of the same piece, not two separate things.

🧩 Formations & lineups Confirmed XIs, not guessed ones. Shapes in and out of possession.
⚔️ Tactical matchups The specific duel or system clash that will likely decide the game.
📊 Projected stats xG, possession, shots - modelled from form, not pulled from thin air.
🔄 Live updates Prediction and analysis updated in real time as the match happens.
Why this exists

Most prediction content is either pure stats or pure opinion.

Stats without tactical context don't explain anything. Opinion without structure is just noise. The aim here is to combine both - readable, visual, and genuinely analytical. Something that holds itself accountable by showing the pre-match prediction alongside the post-match reality every single time.
